Eye of Culture in Family

ECF is an organization dedicated to preventing divorces and child abuse as it promotes family stability, protects children from harm, prevents long-term consequences, supports mental and emotional health, contributes to community well-being, educates the public, and provides legal advocacy, ultimately creating a safer and more compassionate society.

Divorce and child abuse can lead to severe mental and emotional health issues for those involved. Eye of Culture in Family(ECF) offers counseling and therapy services to help individuals cope with and heal from traumatic experiences.

Healthy families and communities go hand in hand. By preventing divorces and child abuse, ECF contributes to the overall well-being and stability of communities, reducing the social and economic costs associated with these issues.

Our Objectives

Reducing divorces

Promote unity among spouses and avoid the increase of divorces.

Morals learning

Teaching the youth about the moral values.

Averting child abuse

Promote child rights and eradicate child abuse.

Gender equality

Promote gender equality and eradicate gender inequality.
